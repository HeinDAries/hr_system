Key trend: Time to fill is worsening (+8d above target in March vs +3d in January). Root cause: Myanmar operational roles take 90+ days due to thin talent pool and hardship hesitancy. Non-Myanmar roles average 52 days, within target. Fix Myanmar specifically, not the overall process.
Pipeline volume: Grew from 11 candidates in January to 24 in March — sourcing activity is working. Conversion to hired has not kept pace, suggesting screening and offer stages are the bottleneck, not sourcing volume.
Offer acceptance: Trending down (75% Jan → 67% Mar). Counter-offers are increasing — need to verify market rates for Myanmar roles are still competitive and consider adding non-cash perks to improve acceptance without exceeding budget.

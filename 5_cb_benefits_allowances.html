C&B Tool 5 — Benefits and Allowances Register

Benefits &
Allowances

The complete reference register for all Valentis Group benefits, allowances, and non-cash entitlements across Laos, Myanmar, Indonesia, and Thailand. The single source of truth for what employees receive beyond base salary.

What This Register Covers
Benefits and allowances are everything an employee receives beyond their base salary. At Valentis, these fall into four categories:

Core Benefits — entitlements that apply to all employees regardless of role or country, including health coverage, annual leave, and life insurance.

Site and Field Allowances — additional compensation for the operational realities of exploration work — remote locations, rotation schedules, hardship conditions.

Statutory Requirements — legally mandated contributions and entitlements in each country. Non-negotiable and non-discretionary.

Discretionary Benefits — additional items provided at company discretion, often used competitively in hard-to-source roles.
Why Benefits Matter to Total Cost
Total Employer Cost
At Valentis, total employer cost typically runs 20–35% above base salary depending on country and role. Indonesia and Thailand are highest due to statutory contribution requirements. This register tracks every component of that cost.
Candidate Communication
Many candidates compare only base salary. When Valentis communicates total rewards — including the cash value of R&R flights, accommodation, meals, and health coverage — the package becomes considerably more competitive.
Compliance Risk
Missing a statutory benefit — a social security contribution, a mandated 13th month, or a legally required leave entitlement — creates employment law risk. This register ensures nothing is missed.

Section 02 — Core Benefits

Core
Benefits

Benefits that apply to all permanent employees across all four countries. These form the baseline of the Valentis benefits package regardless of role, grade, or location.

All-employee entitlementsEvery benefit on this page applies to all permanent employees from their first day of employment unless otherwise noted. Probationary employees have modified entitlements as specified in each item.
Section 03 — Site and Field Allowances

Site &
Field Allowances

Additional compensation for employees working in remote field locations, on rotation schedules, or in designated hardship environments. These are critical for attracting and retaining operational site staff.

Role and location dependentThese allowances are not universal — they apply based on role assignment, site designation, and country. The eligibility matrix (Section 05) shows exactly which roles qualify. Allowances are separate from base salary and are not included in merit increase calculations.
Section 04 — Statutory Requirements

Statutory
Requirements

Legally mandated benefits, contributions, and entitlements in each country. These are floors — Valentis meets or exceeds all statutory requirements. Non-compliance creates criminal and civil liability.

Legal minimum — non-negotiableStatutory benefits cannot be contracted away, reduced, or made conditional on performance. If Valentis offers a benefit that exceeds the statutory minimum (e.g. more annual leave than required), the excess is discretionary. The statutory minimum is always protected.

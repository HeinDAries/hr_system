Rejection templates · post-screen · post-interview · post-offer
Never ghost a candidate. A 3-line rejection is better than silence. In the SEA drilling and mining community, reputation travels fast — how you reject people affects who refers to you next.
Email — Post-Screen Rejection (No Interview)
Subject: Your Application — [Role Title] · BaKuu Holding Dear [Candidate Name], Thank you for your interest in the [Role Title] position at BaKuu Holding and for taking the time to share your background with us. After careful review, we have decided to progress with other candidates whose experience more closely matches our requirements at this time. We appreciate your interest and wish you every success in your search. [Your Name] Group HR Manager, BaKuu Holding
Email — Post-Interview Rejection (Round 1)
Subject: [Role Title] — Update from BaKuu Holding Dear [Candidate Name], Thank you for the time you invested in our conversation for the [Role Title] position. We valued learning more about your background and experience. After careful consideration, we have decided to move forward with another candidate whose experience is a closer match to our specific requirements at this stage. This was a competitive process and the decision was not straightforward. We would like to stay in touch as our operations continue to grow across Southeast Asia — roles in [relevant area] come up regularly and we hope to consider you for future opportunities. [Optional: If you would find brief feedback from the interview helpful, I am happy to share it — please let me know.] We wish you every success. [Your Name] Group HR Manager, BaKuu Holding
Email — Post-Interview Rejection (Final Round)
Subject: [Role Title] — Final Decision · BaKuu Holding Dear [Candidate Name], Thank you for your time throughout our recruitment process for the [Role Title] position. You invested significantly in the process and we genuinely appreciate that. After thorough deliberation, we have made the difficult decision to proceed with another candidate. This was an extremely close decision — you were among the strongest candidates we have interviewed this year. I would personally like to stay in touch. BaKuu Holding is growing and I expect roles at a similar level to open across our SEA operations within the next 6–12 months. With your permission, I will keep your details on file and reach out when something relevant comes up. Thank you again, and I hope we have the opportunity to work together in the future. [Your Name] Group HR Manager, BaKuu Holding

Work permit and visa support letters · official correspondence
All permit request letters must be signed by an authorised company signatory (Country Director or COO) and issued on official company letterhead. These are legal documents — do not send drafts directly to authorities.
Letter — Work Permit Application Support Letter (All Countries)
[COMPANY LETTERHEAD] [Date] To: [Relevant Authority — e.g. Department of Labour and Social Welfare, Laos / Ministry of Labour, Myanmar / Ministry of Manpower, Indonesia / Department of Employment, Thailand] Re: Work Permit Application — [Employee Full Name], Passport No. [Passport Number] Dear Sir/Madam, We write in support of the work permit application for [Employee Full Name], a national of [Country of citizenship], currently holding Passport Number [Number] valid until [expiry date]. [Employee Name] has been offered the position of [Job Title] with [Company Legal Name], a company registered in [Country] under registration number [Company Reg. No.]. The nature of this role requires specialised expertise in [brief description — e.g. mineral exploration drilling operations / financial compliance / multi-country HR management] that is not currently available in the local labour market. We have made reasonable efforts to recruit locally and have been unable to identify suitably qualified candidates for this specific role. The proposed terms of employment are as follows: Position: [Job Title] Contract Duration: [Permanent / Fixed-term: X months] Monthly Salary: [Currency and amount] Work Location: [Site/City/Country] Start Date: [Proposed start date] We respectfully request your approval of this application and undertake to comply with all applicable local labour laws during the period of employment. Please do not hesitate to contact us if further documentation is required. Yours faithfully, [Authorised Signatory Name] [Title — e.g. Country Director / Chief Operating Officer] [Company Name] [Phone / Email]
Letter — Employee Support Letter (Visa Application / Bank / Accommodation)
[COMPANY LETTERHEAD] [Date] To Whom It May Concern, Re: Confirmation of Employment — [Employee Full Name] We confirm that [Employee Full Name], a national of [Country] holding Passport Number [Number], is currently employed by [Company Legal Name] in the position of [Job Title]. Details of employment: Start Date: [Date] Employment Type: [Permanent / Fixed-term] Monthly Salary: [Currency and amount] Work Location: [City/Country] This letter is issued at the employee's request for the purpose of [visa application / bank account opening / accommodation rental / other — specify]. The Company accepts no liability for obligations entered into by the employee in their personal capacity. If further verification is required, please contact us at the details below. Yours faithfully, [Authorised Signatory Name] [Title] [Company Name] [Phone / Email]

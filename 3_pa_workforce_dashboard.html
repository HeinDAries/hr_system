Section 02 — Headcount & Grade Mix

Headcount &
Grade Mix

Headcount by grade and country — current snapshot, trend direction, and target gap. Grade mix tells a structural story: too heavy at junior grades means high training cost; too heavy at senior means succession risk.

Section 03 — Pipeline Health

Pipeline
Health

The G1→G4 driller development pipeline — conversion rates by country, stage velocity, bench strength, and at-risk candidate flags. The most critical operational metric at BaKuu Holding.

Section 04 — Attrition Tracker

Attrition
Tracker

Attrition rate by country and grade — current quarter, rolling 12-month, and trend. Month-3 exit pattern tracking. Voluntary vs involuntary breakdown. Cost of attrition estimate.

Section 05 — Delivery & Cost

Delivery &
Cost

Programme delivery percentage by country against target, and people cost as a percentage of revenue. The two metrics that connect workforce health directly to business performance.

Section 06 — Alert Centre

Alert
Centre

All active workforce alerts in one place — ordered by priority. Alerts are generated from the data entered in the Dashboard Updater. Red alerts require action before the next quarterly COO report. Amber alerts require a monitoring plan.

Section 07 — Dashboard Updater

Dashboard
Updater

Enter the quarter's data here. All dashboard views, KPIs, alerts, and the COO summary update automatically from this single data entry point. Complete all fields — partial entries produce incomplete alerts.

Section 08 — COO Summary View

COO
Summary

A one-page workforce health summary formatted for COO review. Generated from the current dashboard data. All metrics in business language — revenue, cost, operational impact. Copy and paste into the quarterly COO brief.

Flight Risk Indicators
Employees most likely to leave — and why it matters
What Is a Flight Risk?
BaKuu Holding definition — 3 or more risk factors present
High-Risk Factors
  • Rating 4 or 5 for 2+ consecutive years
  • No salary increase in last 12 months
  • No promotion or role expansion in 2+ years
Medium-Risk Factors
  • Critical role (hard to backfill)
  • Below-market compensation
  • Mentioned career concerns in review
Market Context
  • Drillers: High regional demand, 60-90 day replacement
  • Site Managers: Extremely scarce in SEA
  • Finance/HR: 45-60 day replacement typical
